Pinkberry

The Problem
Pinkberry strives to be flexible and accomodating for their customers. With their growing popularity, customers have felt that waiting in line has become a common annoyance when trying to pick up their favorite Pinkberry frozen yogurt.
The Solution
The solution to this problem is to build a Pinkberry Kiosk. A Pinkberry Kiosk will eliminate long lines and allow customers to purchase customized yogurt at the touch of a button. The idea was to make the kiosk visually appealing for all ages and to make the interface simple enough for children to use. The color scheme and fonts were chosen to align with the current Pinkberry designs. This simple interface makes creating your own Pinkberry creation easy and enjoyable.
