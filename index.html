About Me

I've been interested in design my entire life. Starting as young as I can remember, I wanted to be an architect and interior designer. I bought my first sketch book in the 4th grade, and in it I sketched designs for my entire house. Although they were never put to use, they further drove my passion for design. It wasn't until I entered college that I discovered Human Factors Engineering. With Human Factors, I am able to advance my design skills and apply them to real world problems. Pairing Human Factors with Computer Science has given me the ability to design and implement applications. Through my coursework at Tufts and real world experience through clubs and internships, I began to realize how important Human Factors really is. Check out my website to see some of the projects I've worked on.
Thanks for visiting!
