Antiox

Providing health-conscious and health-deprived individuals with access to easy and affordable nutrients that keep the body healthy and the mind stable.

The Problem
Students at highly rigorous academic institutions have a hard time balancing sleep, eating healthy, exercising, and maintaining their grades. As four college students, we felt that our intense schedules and lack of time lead us to behaviors that leave us unable to feel our best. With our diverse backgrounds in computer science, biology, econ, and sociology, we set out to find a solution to this widespread problem.
The Solution
Antiox is an up and coming startup within the healthcare industry that offers a transdermal patch with vitamins and antioxidants in it. Our product offers a mobile application that makes buying vitamin patches convenient and easily understandable. Our goal is to increase the daily lives of students at academically rigorous and stressful institutions in the Greater Boston Area.
Meet the Team

My Role

As project manager, I was responsible with planning and overseeing all aspects of the project. My expertise in executing the user-centered design process allowed me to design a product that was geared toward our target users.

My role as project manager gave me the important experience of learning how to build and maintain a strong team. By overseeing the initiation, planning, execution, and closure of this project, I gained many practical skills that I have since been able to utilize when working on cross-functional teams.
Designs
Our Product
Antiox offers a transdermal patch with vitamins and antioxidants that help students to feel their best at all times. To better cater to individuals in our target market, we have designed a mobile app to provide our product and to make purchasing easier. After surveying individuals in our target audience, we decided to focus our patches on solving three main issues. The first will be an immune and skin care patch. Our third and final patch will be a hangover patch.

Value

The value that Antiox offers students is the ability to maintain a healthy balance between sleep, physical health, mental health, and academic success. Our patch allows vitamins and antioxidants to diffuse across the skin over just a few hours, much faster than other daily vitamins. Antiox gives students the solution to remain healthy while living their not-so healthy lifestyles.
